    Explainable routing

    It decides where things go.
    And it shows its work.

    No black box. Every capture carries a plain-English reason — “looks like a to-do”, “has a time”, “contains a link” — so you always know why something landed where it did, and you can correct it once.

    Split on send

    One mixed capture fans out — action lines become individual reminders, the prose stays together as one note.

    One-tap correction

    Every guess carries its reason and a “Not a to-do?” flip. You are never stuck with the machine's opinion.

    Undo, even after send

    A short grace window holds each capture back. ⌘Z pulls it back before it leaves your machine.

    Updates itself

    Signed builds arrive quietly in the background. Restart when you feel like it — never mid-thought.
